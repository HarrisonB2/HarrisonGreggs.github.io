About

Emerging from the Covid pandemic, filmmaker and video editor Harrison Greggs made his first short horror film, “What the Eyes Can’t See.” Harrison studies video production and filmmaking in pursuit of his Audio-Video Media Production Associate of Arts degree at Howard Community College (Spring 2022). His experience includes working with the editing software packages Adobe Premiere Pro, Sony Vegas Pro, and iMovie; and he is certified in Adobe Photoshop. He is working on his next short film, “Nostalgia,” which is scheduled for release in 2022. In addition, Harrison works editing basketball highlight reels, sports montages, and cinematic shot reels. His experience includes working behind the camera doing cinematography and photography on his projects. Harrison works and lives in Columbia, Maryland.
